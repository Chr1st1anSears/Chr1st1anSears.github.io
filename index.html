American Junkie

Do people ever really change? Can a junkie truly recover from addiction? Most “quit lit” stories are just that—stories about quitting, about sobering up. American Junkie goes deeper. It probes the causes and conditions that lie beneath the wreckage of addiction and the promise of sobriety. Through the eyes of Jack Sutherland, readers will see what it takes to mend a shattered soul.
